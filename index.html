[HRI 2026] Can You Help Me? The Influence of Robot Requests for Help on Child-Robot Connection

  • Contributed to study design, including task flow, experimental conditions, and behavioral measures
  • Led autonomous robot system development for emotional expression and collaborative gameplay on a Misty II robot, including algorithm design, speech interaction pipeline, TCP-based gameplay integration, and prompt engineering
  • Conducted a between-subjects study (N=100) with 6–10-year-old children, showing that a robot’s asking for help builds trust between a robot and a child, and that expressing emotional vulnerability creates further feelings of connection

[HRI 2025] Exploring Robot Personality Traits and Their Influence on User Affect and Experience

  • Developed a method using large language models to generate dialogue consistent with different personality traits, providing a scalable and flexible approach for future robot personality research
  • Programmed a teleoperated NAO robot with three distinct personalities (expected, high extraversion, high neuroticism)
  • Conducted a between-subjects study (N=66), showing that both high extraversion and high neuroticism personalities significantly enhanced participants’ emotional states, with high extraversion rated most enjoyable

Projects

[Course Project] Gesture-Controlled Home Companion Robot

We trained a computer vision model to detect user hand gestures and implemented an inverse kinematics algorithm on a TurtleBot 4, enabling the robot to recognize gestures and perform corresponding tasks. Depending on the detected gesture, the robot can search for and retrieve a target item for the user, dance with the user, or move to a corner to rest and wait for the next command.

Skills: Python, ROS 2, PyTorch

Video

[Course Project] SafeWalk: A Wearable Safety Assistant for Blind Users

We built a wearable belt designed to support safer navigation for blind users. The system uses ultrasonic sensors, buzzers, and motors to detect nearby obstacles and guide users toward safer paths. It also uses an accelerometer and Bluetooth communication to send alerts to a friend or caregiver when a fall is detected or when the user manually sends an emergency alert.

Skills: C++, Arduino, Circuit Design, Soldering

Video

[Course Project] VR Diver Interface

We developed a VR diver interface in Unity that supports one-handed gesture controls, accessible visual and audio feedback, and confirmation steps for safety-critical actions.

Skills: C#, Unity, VR, UI/UX Design
